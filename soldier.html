Support of the people was crucial, a task at which Shivaji Maharaj excelled. He commanded some of the finest soldiers, renowned for their exceptional qualities:
Courageous and Loyal: Shivaji Maharaj's soldiers were distinguished by their unwavering courage and steadfast loyalty to their king.
Defenders of Dharma: Beyond mere warriors, they upheld righteousness (dharma), defending their land and people with valor and honor.
Resourceful and Agile: Known for their resourcefulness and agility in battle, Shivaji's soldiers adapted swiftly to changing tactics and terrain.
Diverse and United: Coming from diverse backgrounds, Shivaji's soldiers were unified by a shared mission: safeguarding their homeland from invaders.
Admired and Respected: Revered by both allies and adversaries, Shivaji's soldiers earned admiration for their discipline, strategic prowess, and unwavering commitment.
These qualities defined the soldiers who fought under the banner of Shivaji Maharaj, leaving behind a legacy of bravery and resilience in Indian history. Many names among them are remembered for their supreme sacrifice for Swarajya.

BAJI PASALKAR
  • ONE OF THE CLOSEST PERSON OF SHIVAJI MAHARAJ
  • FIRST GENERAL WHO SACRIFICED HIS FOR SWARAJYA
BAJI PRABHU DESHPANDE
  • ONE OF THE PERSON WHO KNOW TO FIGHT WITH DANDPATTA

  • REMEMBERED FOR HIS STAND BY FIGHTING AGAINST 10000 SOLDIERS WITH 300 MARATHAS IN BATTLE OF PAWANKHIND
BAHIRJI NAIK
  • HEAD OF THE SPY DEPARTMENT

  • HE LAID BLUEPRINT FOR VARIOUS ACTION TAKEN BY SHIVAJI MAHARAJ
TANHAJI MALUSARE
  • CHILDHOOD FRIEND AND OFTEN KNOWN AS RIGHT HAND MAN OF SHIVAJI MAHARAJ

  • KNOWN FOR VALOUR SHOWN BY HIM IN BATTLE OF KONDHANA AND KEPT TASK OF SWARAJYA FIRST INSTEAD ATTENDING HIS SON WEDDING
YESAJI KANK
  • ONE OF THE CLOSEST FRIEND ALONG WITH TANHAJI

  • HE IS KNOWN FOR THE ELEPHANT FIGHT WHICH HAPPENED IN HYDERABAD WHICH WAS DONE TO TEST ABILITY OF SOLDIER

NETOJI PALKAR
  • ALSO KNOWN AS PRATI SHIVAJI

  • HE IS KNWON FOR HIS VALOUR. HE WAS ONE OF THE PERSON FOR WHOM SHIVAJI MAHARAJ DONE SHUDDHI PROCESS TO REVERT HIM BACK TO HINDUISM
KANHOJI ANGRE
  • ALSO KNWON AS Samudratla Shivaji

  • HE WAS KNOWN FOR MAKING MARATHA NAVY STRONGER AND POWERFUL
HIROJI INDULKAR
  • HE WAS THE MAIN ARCHITECTURE OF MARATHA EMPIRE

  • FORTS LIKE RAIGARH, VIJAYDURG, PRATAPGARH AND VARIOUS STRUCTURES WERE BUILT BY HIM
PRATAPRAO GUJJAR
  • HE WAS FIRST SARSENAPATI OF MARATHA EMPIRE.

  • HE WAS KNWON FOR DILER KHAN INCIDENT WHERE HE LEFT HIM AFTER CAPTIVE WITHOUT SHIVAJI MAHARAJ CONSENT. AFTER HIS ANGRY NOTE TO HIM HE CARRIED 7 SOLIDERS TO CAMP WHERE DILER KHAN WAS THERE AND SACRIFICED HIS LIFE
HAMBIRRAO MOHITE
  • HE WAS SECOND SARSENAPATI OF MARATHA EMPRE AND HE WAS UNCLE OF SHIVAJI MAHARAJ

  • HE IS KNOWN FOR VARIOUS CAMPAIGNS NOT MUCH DURING SHIVAJI MAHARAJ REIGN BUT DURING SAMBHAJI MAHARAJ REIGN
