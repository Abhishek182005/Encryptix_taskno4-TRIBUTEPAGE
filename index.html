THE WARRIOR KING

CHHATRAPATI SHIVAJI MAHARAJ

  • Father of Indian Navy
  • Known for Guerilla warfare
  • Creating Hindavi Swaraj
  • Inspired people to fight against atrocities
  • Still known as a source of inspiration

SOME MAJOR SAYINGS

Mahatma Gandhi:
"Shivaji is an inspiration to every Indian, to rise against injustice and fight for freedom."
Source: Collected Works of Mahatma Gandhi, Volume 15
Swami Vivekananda:
"Shivaji was one of the greatest national saviours who emancipated our society."
Source: Complete Works of Swami Vivekananda, Volume 4
Rabindranath Tagore:
"Shivaji possessed no kingdom, no capital, no permanent residence. He possessed nothing. But his spirit was unconquerable."
Source: Rabindranath Tagore's Letters
Bal Gangadhar Tilak:
"Shivaji was not only a great warrior but also a great administrator and a man of high moral character."
Source: Writings and Speeches of Lokmanya Tilak
Vinayak Damodar Savarkar:
"Shivaji's life is the greatest epic of modern India."
Source: Life of Barrister Savarkar
